Our Departments

Where you will find the best medical care ever

Neurology

Specialized clinician researchers in the Department of Neurology in order to help in movement disorders, stroke, neuro-oncology, multiple sclerosis.

Surgical

Operating rooms are equipped with the brand-newest operating tables,all necessary modern neurosurgical facilities, surgical microscopes, intraoperative ultrasound unit and monitoring of brain and spinal cord.

Opthalmology

The mission of the Department of Ophthalmology is to provide our patients with quality, state-of-the-art ophthalmic care in all subspecialties of ophthalmology.

Traumatology

the Department of Traumatology is established by young physicians from Departments of Surgery, Orthopedics and Emergency Medicine. It is a new force in traumatology. It aims at providing trauma service 24 hours around the clock, so that patients sustaining major trauma are no longer helpless.

Nuclear Magnetic

Our Medical Imaging Department provides diagnostic, ultrasound, CAT scan, nuclear medicine, MRI and mammography services.

Cardiology

The Department of Cardiology which comprises specialists of various cardiac subspecialties, provides a broad range of services in the diagnosis and management of all types of heart diseases.
